About Ereft

Friendship at the peak.

Ereft Hiking is an Ethiopian hiking and tour community based in Addis Ababa. We organize friendly, safe, and memorable group trips for people who want to explore Ethiopia's nature, culture, and outdoor beauty.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our hiking trips.

Comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, a small backpack, sunscreen, a hat, and a water bottle. We provide refreshments and packed water, but extra snacks are welcome.

Our trips are designed for all fitness levels. Routes range from easy walks to moderate hikes. If you have health concerns, let us know before departure so we can accommodate you.

Yes. Round-trip transport from Addis Ababa is included in all packages. We use comfortable coaster buses and meet at Wabi Shebelle Hotel in the Mexico area.

If we have already purchased a spot or registered you for the trip, cancellations are not possible in most cases. Contact us as early as possible to see if any option is available.

Native/Local (prices in ETB) is for Ethiopian residents. Foreigner (prices in USD) is for international visitors. Both have the same two options:

Day Trip — You come, hike, and return the same day. Transport, meals, and guide included.

Overnight — You go, sleep at the destination, and come back the next day. Includes camping gear, meals, and activities.
